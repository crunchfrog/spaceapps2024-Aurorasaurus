Aurora Collapse

About the Game

Aurora Collapse is an adrenaline-pumping space adventure where players must escape the deadly solar flares unleashed by a catastrophic solar event. Navigate through cosmic landscapes, avoid intense radiation, and use your spaceship's abilities to survive.

Stunning Visuals

Upgrade Your Ship

Engaging Storyline

Unravel the mystery behind the solar collapse while racing against time to save your crew and yourself.
